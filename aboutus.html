The Story Behind Swadra

Swadra is not just a brand, it is a personal realization. My name is Ajay Singh, and I began to notice that in today's India, people are facing serious health issues much earlier in life. By the age of 60-65, many are already battling diseases. When I looked deeper, I realized the truth: the food we eat today is no longer truly pure. Excessive pesticides and chemicals have silently become a part of our daily diet.

That realization led to the birth of Swadra Organics, not to sell products, but to restore trust. I personally ensure sourcing and quality, offering products that are either pesticide-free or contain only minimal, safe levels. This journey is not easy, but when it comes to health, there can be no compromise.

If it's not pure, it's not Swadra.

Ajay Singh
